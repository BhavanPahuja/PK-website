Portfolio

Here's a short overview of my work over the last 33 years working in various capacities in the

Rural, Panchayat, and Irrigation Departments of the Punjab Government.

  • 2018-Current

    Working as Sub-Divisional Officer (Planning) in Department of Rural Development & Panchayats of Punjab Government. The job involves planning of the various schemes of the department at State Headquarters. The job also involves preparation of drawings, checking of estimates and preparation of Detailed Project Reports. The main projects undertaken during this tenure were about Solid Waste and Liquid Management in the States’ villages.

  • 2017-2018

    Worked as Sub-Divisional Officer (Panchayati Raj), SAS Nagar. During this period various Building and other Development Works such as construction of Streets & Drains in the villages were undertaken. The major project undertaken during this period was Construction of Auditorium costing Rs. 20.00 Crores (Approx. 3,300,000 Cad) having a capacity of 800 persons. The building also has a basement parking covering an area of 20,000 sft.

  • 2007-2017

    Worked as Assistant Engineer. Was instrumental in construction of various buildings, renovation of ponds, construction of streets & drains in the villages. Also successfully involved in running and maintenance of the building of the department’s State Headquarters (Vikas Bhawan) having an approximate area of 2,00,000 sft) from 2012 to 2017 apart from maintaining Guest House in the premises.

  • 2000-2007

    Worked as Junior Engineer. Was entrusted with the execution of development works (such as building Schools, Community Centres, Civil & Veterinary Dispensaries, Streets & Drains, Culverts, Sewerage System etc.) in the Rural Areas of the State of the Punjab.

  • 1999

    Was given additional charge of Sub-Divisional Officer and was involved in contruction/inspection of various development works in the Rural Areas of the State of Punjab.

  • 1993-1999

    Worked as Junior Engineer. Was entrusted with the execution of development works (such as building Schools, Community Centres, Civil & Veterinary Dispensaries, Streets & Drains, Culverts, Sewerage System etc.) in the Rural Areas of the State of the Punjab.

  • 1993

    Worked as Junior Engineer in Bhakra Beas Management Board. The nature of duties involved the settlement observation of the world’s second highest gravity dam viz; Bhakra Dam. Have the privilege of handling and using the surveying instruments manufactured by WILD for checking the behaviour of Dam and its adjoining hills with the increase and decrease of water level in the dam.

  • 1991-1993

    Worked as Junior Engineer in the Canal Lining wing of the Punjab Irrigation Department. The work entrusted was brick lining of the canals during the closure of the canal during harvesting season. The work used to time bound as very approximately a fortnight period was given to complete the lining work due to start of sowing season immediately after harvesting is over.

  • 1986-1991

    Worked as Supervisor Inspection & Quality Control. Was instrumental in inspection of Sutlej Yamuna Link Canal. The works inspected were of diversified nature involving canal lining, super-passage and aqueducts, bridges etc.
